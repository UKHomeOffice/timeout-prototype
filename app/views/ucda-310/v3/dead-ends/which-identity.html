What is a biometric residence card?

You have a biometric residence card if it says either:

  • ‘Residence card’ at the top
  • ‘Residence card of a family member of a union citizen’ at the bottom
  • ‘Residence card issued under EEA regulations’ at the bottom
  • ‘EU right to reside’ on the back
  • ‘Permanent residence’, ‘EU family member’ or ‘Derivative residence’ at the bottom
What is a biometric residence permit?

You have a biometric residence permit if it shows your:

  • name, date and place of birth
  • fingerprints and a photo of your face (your biometric information)
  • immigration status and any conditions of your stay
