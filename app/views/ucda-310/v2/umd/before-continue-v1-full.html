Before you continue

Use this service to prove your identity or provide evidence when updating your information with the Home office.

Complete the following steps:

1. Provide a new photo of yourself

Your will need to:

  1. Read our guidelines on taking a photo of yourself
  2. Take and upload the photo.
  3. Check the photo meets our guidelines.
  4. Submit the photo.

You can either take a selfie or get someone to take a photo of you using a smartphone, tablet or digital camera.

If you prefer, you can get a digital photo from a ophoto shop or photo booth.

2. Provide a new photo of your identity document

Your will need to:

  1. Read our guidelines on taking a photo of your identity document.
  2. Take and upload the photo.
  3. Check the photo meets our guidelines.
  4. Submit the photo.

You can use a smartphone, tablet or digital camera to take a photo of your identity document or a scanner to make a copy.
