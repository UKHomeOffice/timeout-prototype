Document information

The photo must show:

  • all your personal details (including the photo of you)
  • all 4 corners of the passport

The machine-readable zone is at the bottom of your passport and on the same page as your personal information.

Quality

The photo must be:

  • clear and in focus
  • in colour
  • unaltered by effect or filters
  • without glare or reflections
  • horizontal (landscape)
  • an original photo - photocopies are not allowed
